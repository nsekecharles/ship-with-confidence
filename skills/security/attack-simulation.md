# Attack Simulation

Mentally simulate realistic attacker behaviors against the change.

## What to check
- Injection (SQL/NoSQL/command/template)
- SSRF, CSRF, XSS
- Rate limiting and brute force
- Deserialization and file upload handling

## Prompts
- "Act as an attacker and enumerate abuse cases for this feature."
- "What is the worst input that could be sent here and what happens?"

## Deliverable
- Top 5 attack paths + mitigations
